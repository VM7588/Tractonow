🎯 Our Mission

TractoNow was built to solve a real problem faced by farmers across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh — affordable, timely access to agricultural equipment. We connect farmers with local equipment owners through a smart platform powered by AI demand prediction, live GPS tracking, and group booking — making modern AgriTech accessible to every farmer, regardless of technical knowledge.

⚡ Key Features

🤖

AI Demand Prediction

Machine Learning predicts high-demand seasons so farmers can book early and save money.

👥

Group Booking

Multiple farmers share one equipment — cost splits automatically. A unique feature that reduces individual burden.

📍

Live GPS Tracking

Real-time equipment location with ETA. No more waiting and wondering where your tractor is.

🌦️

Weather Intelligence

5-day weather forecast based on your registered village/district with farming safety advice.

🗣️

Multilingual Voice

Telugu, Hindi, English voice input — designed for farmers who are not tech-savvy.

💳

Easy Payments

UPI, Card and Net Banking support with booking history and transaction tracking.
